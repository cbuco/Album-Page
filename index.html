No Better Than This

John Mellencamp

August 17, 2010

No Better Than This is a simple, no-frills, American folk album. It was recorded in various locations throughout the United States, entirely at historic studios. The album's unpolished sound is part of its charm.

Band Members

John Mellencamp - Vocals and Acoustic Guitar
Andy York - Acoustic and Electric Guitars
Marc Ribot - Electric Guitar and Banjo
T Bone Burnnett - Acoustic and Electric Guitars
David Roe - Bass
Jay Bellerose - Drums and Percussion
Miriam Sturm - Violin
